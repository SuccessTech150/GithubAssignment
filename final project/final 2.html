lang="en" What is Afrobeats?

Live Bands!

Afrobeat is a genre where live bands are heavily incorporated! The brass and rhythm section are emphasized to help give it a signature sound.The sound is powerful which which energizes the crowd and makes everyone dance! Live bands will always be at the center of this art!


Adressing Politics

Afrobeat always had a very strong political position. The music talked about the state of politics in Africa and abroad! It was used to shine a light on the challenges from the Nigerian government and inspire change from the people.


The Growth

Afrobeats is one of the most recognisable sounds currently! It has grown in large part to the joyful/upbeat feeling you get from listening to it. The streams have gone up 500% since 2017 worldwide. This genre is here to stay and the growth constantly caters to that belief!


The Way It Makes You Feel
